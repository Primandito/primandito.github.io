About Me

I am a dedicated bug hunter who began my vulnerability research journey in November 2025, building upon a foundational understanding of web technologies and security concepts. My primary focus is on Bugcrowd Vulnerability Disclosure Programs (VDPs) for web applications, where I assist organizations in identifying and remediating security weaknesses before they can be abused by real-world attackers.

I approach security research with a structured and methodical mindset, combining in-depth manual testing with targeted automation to uncover vulnerabilities in web applications and APIs. My work emphasizes understanding application logic, attack surfaces, and real-world exploitability rather than relying solely on automated tools.

I strongly value responsible disclosure and view bug hunting as a way to contribute meaningfully to a safer internet ecosystem. Through consistency, continuous learning, and strong attention to detail, I have successfully earned recognition in multiple Hall of Fames on Bugcrowd, reflecting both the impact and quality of my findings.
